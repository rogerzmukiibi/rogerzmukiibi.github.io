I have had the idea of having a blog for a really long time. Tried word-press and many others but I could not get past 3 posts. This is my second here so let me not brag.

I am going to keep it short though. Besides the first post which I was using to set up the site, this is the post I can let some one read. I will leave them up any way. 

How I am pulling of this blog thing? The question I am answering. I hope my future self can look at it and know what I was doing. 

I am using git-hub pages to host. Let me not go so fast. To start with I was reading a post form Andrej on how to study or something... not sure. Then I realised he has also had a spin of blogs and he settled for git hub pages. Guess what. ITS FREE.
 I jumped straight to his repository, copied it and had something running. I was in a really bad shape, and it still is. But I was very happy. I am still pleased by the progress.

So I use Git hub pages and the website has to be a public repository (that's all I know, just incase I am wrong).  

I had a hard time selecting a logo for the site and ended up getting that fro Andrej's. What  shame but regardless. Can you imagine, I was excited to have the blog but I was busy so I never looked at it for a week. Where is my excitement really. 

Apparently the posts are to be written in markdown. Guess what. That means a resurrection of my obsidian knowledge which I had gotten but failed to use. So I am writing in obsidian in an old vault that I had pushed to git hub. I am excited to use it again for sure. 

What am i forgetting. Yeah, I am keeping the volt in the draft/ that I ignore so it is not pushed to git hub but if I get time I might switch to google docs when writing. but I am still giving obsidian a short. Quick note no AI hear 😆 
Hopefully next week I have read the jekyllrb documentation and put up a better UI but sofar so good. 
Enjoy...
