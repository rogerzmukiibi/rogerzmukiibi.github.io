# Masculinity
2025-02-13

Yesterday I attended a talk called the Junction at [Bugala](https://bugala.org/) on the topic: Masculinity and I noted down a few things to keep for the future. 
## What is a man?  
Leader, protector, provider and society builder.  
  
### Man as a leader  
- Establishes rules of conduct  
- Expenditure and investment of money  
- where to live  
- educational planning  
- upbringing children in faith ( children are more likely to keep their faith if a father is prayerful)  
NB: Value the opinion of the wife, she brings in a unique perspective.  
  
### Man as a protector  
- Danger (sexual assault, evil influences, hard elements)  
- Difficulties ( financial hardships, pressure from relatives)  
- Taking on the masculine roles in the house. Do the hard things 💪🏾  
NB: Spoil your wife more than your kids😂  
  
### Man as a society builder  
- identify problems in society and find solutions  
- make a world a better place  
  
### Man as a provider  
- Financial support ( progress, manage and worry about the money)  
- Fidelity ( loyalty, keep the marital commitment)  
NB: A man's conduct must be above suspicion.  
  
  
Further references: [into the breach](https://www.kofc.org/en/campaigns/into-the-breach.html)
